Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Harmony?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're ready to start exploring our music library.

Can I upload my own music to Harmony?

Yes, we encourage artists to share their work! After creating an account, go to your dashboard and click the "Upload" button. You'll be guided through the process of adding your music to our platform.

How can I report a technical issue?

If you're experiencing technical difficulties, please contact our support team using the form on this page. Be sure to include details about your device, browser, and a description of the issue for faster resolution.
